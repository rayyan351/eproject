About our ENT Services


Dealing with ear, nose, and throat related issues with the help of top experts in the profession.

The ENT Department deals with conditions and disorders of the ear, nose, and throat and related areas of the neck and head. Our professionals treat a wide range of usual to complex ENT diseases in both pediatrics and adult patients. The treatment procedures for Endoscopic Sinus, Ear tube, and other surgeries such as Microlaryngoscopy, tympanoplasty with ossicular reconstruction, and refractive procedures are performed consistently. Our ENT Doctors are experts in repairing the tissue damage as well as restoring the affected areas. The medical experts ensure that affected patients enjoy the quality of life by going to their everyday lives and be effectively functional.

Advantage:

Some of the issues treated successfully by our ENT professionals are listed here:

  • Voice, Airway, and swallowing disorders.
  • Enlarged adenoids and tonsils.
  • Deviated septum.
  • Vertigo and Dizziness.
  • Ear Infection.
  • Hearing disorders.
  • Labyrinthitis.
  • Nosebleeds.
  • Sleep Apnea.
  • Pediatric Ear, Nose, and throat diseases.
  • Runny Nose.
  • Snoring
  • Allergy Disorders.
