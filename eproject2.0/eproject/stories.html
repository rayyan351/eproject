​​Ismail Comes Back Stronger​​​

Ismail Ahmed was diagnosed with a brain condition called Arteriovenous Malformation (AVM). AVM is a tangle of abnormal blood vessels connecting the arteries and veins in the brain. Ismail underwent neurosurgery, from Cambridge Memorial Hospital, followed by regular physiotherapy to help him recover his motor skills.

From Paralysis To Remarkable Recovery​​​

Dr Shabbir Ahmed Naeem had no apparent medical complaint before he suffered paralysis attack. Dr Shabbir, an associate professor at SIUT, was rushed to the Cambridge Memorial Hospital after suffering a spinal injury. The experienced team at the CMH diagnosed him correctly and treated him swiftly.

​​Early Detection can Save Lives ​​​

Fareed Farooq's mother was diagnosed with stage one breast cancer. She underwent successful surgery to remove the cancer in 2018 at Cambridge Memorial Hospital. The surgery would have completely eradicated the cancer and eliminate the risk of it growing any further.





​​Do Not Lose Hope And Be Brave​​​

Dayem was diagnosed with lymphocytic leukaemia - cancer of the blood and bone marrow in which lymphocytes (a form of white blood cells) become affected. He was given a comprehensive treatment plan, quality care and was free of cancer within 3 years.

​​Zaim's Journey ​​​

Dr. Heeramani Lohana is a children's physician at the CMH. Zaim's lungs were not fully developed at the time of his birth and he had to be kept under observation for seven days. Looking back, it all looks like a miracle of Allah.
