Our Centres


Cancer Centre

CMH has an innovative model of cancer care that ensures a patient’s care needs are met. The hospital’s oncology program provides a full range of services, including in-patient and outpatient diagnosis, treatments, management of complex symptoms, and supportive and palliative care services. Please note that the Oncology Program can no longer accept referrals for patients with hematologic malignancies (i.e., lymphoma, myeloma, leukemia, etc.). Patients with these diseases will be treated at the Grand River Regional Cancer Centre. Our program includes: an inpatient unit an outpatient Oncology Medical Day Clinic Our goal is to provide holistic, integrated cancer services close to home for the residents of our community. Oncology care at Cambridge Memorial Hospital is associated with the Grand River Regional Cancer Centre (GRRCC) and LHIN partners to support our network of linked professionals and services for cancer patients and their families.

Diagnostic Centre

Diagnostic and testing services at Cambridge Memorial Hospital include tests, x-rays (diagnostic imaging), the operation of a cardiorespiratory unit for testing heart and lung functions, infection control and the operation of a laboratory and a pharmacy.

Heart Centre

Here in the Department of Cardiology we focus in providing the most comprehensive diagnostic and therapeutic services to adult patients helping them get relieved from any kind of cardiovascular diseases. We welcome everyone undergoing this distress to visit our department of Cardiology for guidance and treatment, because only early diagnostic can help you save your precious life from fatal risk. We have the most professional and dedicated staff for the most comprehensive cardiology treatment due to which we are ranked among the best in the country. We aim to provide our patients with the best services and treatments, to make them feel no less than a family member

Community Health Centre

The Community Health Centre (CHC) offers clinics with specialized Family Medicine​ and some specialty doctors who provide compassionate and affordable health care for the entire family, not only in sickness, but also routine check ups for good health. The Family Medicine service includes a wide range of services for all ages for the entire the family, including: Vaccinations for infants, young children and adults including, but not limited to, Hepatitis Health promotion and screening for high blood pressure, hepatitis, diabetes, cholesterol, weight management, smoking cessation etc. Family planning services including the placement of an Intra-Uterine Contraceptive Device (IUCD) Women's health services including maternity care, pap smears and breast examinations Minor surgical procedures (e.g. circumcision, incision and drainage, small excisions, toe-nail removal, skin biopsy, wart removal and joint injection) Counselling services for self-relaxation, pain management and care for the terminally ill and their families Pre employment screening and annual check-ups for employees and others
