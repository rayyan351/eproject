Research and Skills Development Center



Established in 1996 as a Skills Lab and flourished to a full fledge facility, Research and Skills Development Centre (RSDC) proudly offers certified training courses in life saving techniques, not only to LNH doctors and staff but also to other institutions and organizations, supervised by certified and experienced instructors. The courses are conducted in line with international guidelines and on regular basis. The centre is licensed by American Heart Association (AHA) as an international training centre. We are ISO 9001-2015 certified and PMDC accredited for CME credit hours. It is a well-equipped and purposely built simulation centre to cater the needs of latest Medical education. Our qualified and experienced instructors also include American Heart Association certified instructors.




Services

  • Emergency Cardiovascular Care (Resuscitation) Skills
  • Clinical Skills for Post graduate Medical Trainees
  • Undergraduate clinical Skills Training (MBBS, Medical Technology, Physical therapy, Diagnostic & Lab Sciences and Nursing)
  • Skills for Professional Nurses (& other Allied Health Professionals)
  • Skills for Technicians & OPD Attendants
  • Emergency Response Skills for Non Healthcare
  • Onsite trainings

Facilities

RSDC is a large and well-equipped facility with a variety of specialized basic and advanced training mannequins and instruments. The centre can accommodate more than hundred participants at a time. To facilitate small group learning, there are classrooms with multimedia projectors and a computer lab. There is a conference room for trainers and faculty, a simulated Operation theatre for animal / cadaver workshops with real anaesthesia machine, suction machine, Diathermy, OT lights, OT table and dissection table, and a tissue lab (for animal tissue) with work stations for hands on skills practice.



Frequently Asked Questions



Most of the courses are offered for the healthcare practitioners and the students. The First and CPR courses though are available for general public and lay men. The non-healthcare professional and students may also get benefit from them.

Select the course from available list. Submit the filled registration forms along with the copy of NIC or employee/student card and payment receipt.

RSDC is a fully equipped and developed resource centre with a whole team of dedicated staff for this purpose including specialized and trained instructors. Customized courses may be offered depending on the availability of the resources.

Usually the successful participants of the courses are awarded with the completion certificates of two years validity. There are though some courses which do not have a conditional validity.

The basic workshops take almost a full day to complete. The advanced courses may be of two to three days.

8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. from Monday through Saturday with Half day on Saturdays
